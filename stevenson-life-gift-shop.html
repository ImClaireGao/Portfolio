Stevenson Life Gift shop Website

A digital platform to promote the Stevenson Cannery historical site, featuring contents from the simulation game Stevenson Life.

  • ROLE: Graphic Designer | Web Designer
  • 2023
  • HTML | CSS
  • PHOTOSHOP

Our team CCCA, developed Steveston Life, a life simulation game designed to engage players and promote interest in fishing history through interactive and educational experiences. Players can earn points in-game for discounts at the gift shop, encouraging visits to the cannery site. The website serves as a platform for game-related merchants and special offers.
