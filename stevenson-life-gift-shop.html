Stevenson Life Gift shop Website

A digital platform to promote the Stevenson Cannery historical site, featuring contents from the simulation game Stevenson Life.

Brief

Our Team CCCA, developed Steveston Life in Q1 2023, a life simulation game designed to engage players and promote interest in fishing history through interactive and educational experiences. Players can earn points in-game for discounts at the gift shop, encouraging visits to the cannery site.

I was responsible for user research and graphic design, creating a game map, activity infographic, and one mini-game for the Stevenson Life.

The following year, I created a one-page website connecting game-related merchants with special offers for players, aiming to increase visitor engagement and help them understand the significance of fishing history on Canadaâ€™s West Coast.
