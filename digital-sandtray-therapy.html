This project replicates the creative experience of a physical sandtray, allowing children to place and interact with virtual objects in a simulated sandbox. It offers 8 themed environments, over 100 miniature objects for storytelling, and a creation tool for therapists to add custom assets. Players can customize avatars and miniature colors, while immersive touring ensures a truly engaging experience.
