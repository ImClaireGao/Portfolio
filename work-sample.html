Hey! I Am Yogost CA & Molly Tea CA

Brand Campaigns and Visual Design for Online and Offline Marketing.

Overview

Hey! I Am Yogost CA and Molly Tea CA are fast-growing beverage brands expanding across Canada. They needed consistent marketing campaigns to drive store openings, seasonal promotions, and customer engagement.

In May 2025, I joined the team as Graphic Designer & Marketing Coordinator, I was responsible for the design of online/offline campaign posters, vouchers, and merchandise, and collaborating with local print shops to ensure production quality.

  • MY ROLE

    Graphic Desgner & Marketing Coordinator

  • MY CREW

    1 Marketing Manager, 2 Graphic Designers

  • Timeline

    May 2025 - Present

  • Tools

    ▪ Illustrator

    ▪ Photoshop

    ▪ InDesign

    ▪ Canva

    ▪ Wordpress

    ▪ Microsoft/Google Office

  • IMPACTS

    ▪ Successfully helped launch the grand opening of the Molly Tea Edmonton store, achieving a first-day GMV of $31,500.

    ▪ Resulted in a 296% increase in views for IG story, with 64% of the viewers being non-followers.
